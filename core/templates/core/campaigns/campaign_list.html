Campaign Status Guide

Draft

Campaign is being prepared. Has email content and recipients. Can be activated or sent immediately.

Active

Campaign is currently sending emails. Can be paused or will complete when all emails are sent.

Paused

Campaign is temporarily stopped. Can be resumed or sent immediately from current state.

Completed

All emails have been sent. Campaign cannot be modified further. Can be duplicated for reuse.
